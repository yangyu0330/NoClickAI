NoClick AI

Data Deletion

Last updated: 2026-05-11

Delete Your Account In The App

Sign in to NoClick AI, open the account panel, choose Account Delete, and enter your account email to confirm. This deletes your NoClick AI account record, active sessions, connected provider tokens, OAuth state, automation runs, audit logs, and workspace state stored by NoClick AI.

Disconnect Google Or Other Providers

You can disconnect a provider from the app without deleting the whole account. Disconnecting removes the stored OAuth token for that provider and stops new automations from using it.

What Is Not Deleted Automatically

NoClick AI cannot delete external items that were already created inside third-party services, such as sent Gmail messages or Google Calendar events. Delete those items directly in the connected service if needed.

Manual Requests

If you cannot sign in, contact the NoClick AI operator through the support address configured in the Google OAuth consent screen and include the account email to delete.
